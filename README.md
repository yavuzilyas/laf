## 🚀 Vision | Vizyon

**English:**
LAF is more than a content platform — it's a digital sanctuary for radical ideas. Built on the principles of decentralization, privacy, and censorship resistance, we empower writers, thinkers, and activists to publish without compromise. No tracking. No gatekeepers. Just pure expression.

**Türkçe:**
LAF sadece bir içerik platformu değil — radikal fikirler için dijital bir sığınak. Merkeziyetsizlik, gizlilik ve sansür direnci ilkeleri üzerine inşa edilen bu platform, yazarların, düşünürlerin ve aktivistlerin ödün vermeden yayın yapmasını sağlar. Takip yok. Bekçi yok. Sadece saf ifade.

## ⚡ What Makes LAF Different | LAF'ı Farklı Kılan Nedir?

### 🔐 Privacy by Design | Tasarım Gereği Gizlilik
- **No email required** — Anonymous publishing with cryptographic identities
- **Self-sovereign identities** — Users control their own data via mnemonic-based accounts
- **Zero tracking** — No analytics, no cookies, no surveillance

### 📝 Professional Publishing Suite | Profesyonel Yayıncılık Araçları
- **Rich text editor** powered by Tiptap with collaborative editing (Yjs + Liveblocks)
- **Multi-language articles** with seamless translation workflows
- **Version control** for every article — never lose your work
- **Markdown & PDF export** for content portability

### 🌐 Decentralized Architecture | Merkeziyetsiz Mimarisi
- **PostgreSQL + MongoDB hybrid** for structured and flexible data
- **S3-compatible storage** for media assets
- **Real-time collaboration** infrastructure
- **Horizontal scaling ready**

### 💰 Integrated Support Economy | Entegre Destek Ekonomisi
- **Direct donations** via Monero (XMR) — privacy-preserving cryptocurrency
- **Badge system** for contributor recognition
- **No platform fees** — 100% of donations go to creators

### 🛡️ Community Moderation | Topluluk Moderasyonu
- **Decentralized moderation** — community-driven content curation
- **Transparent reporting** system
- **Block & follow** mechanisms for user control
- **Event sourcing** for audit trails

## 🎨 Design Philosophy | Tasarım Felsefesi

```
"The interface should disappear, leaving only the ideas"
"Arayüz kaybolmalı, geriye sadece fikirler kalmalı"
```

- **Dark-first aesthetic** — Easy on the eyes, day or night
- **Motion design** — Svelte Motion for fluid, purposeful animations
- **Accessibility** — WCAG 2.1 AA compliant
- **Mobile-native feel** — Responsive without compromise
- **Internationalization** — Full i18n support (TR/EN ready for more)
